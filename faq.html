Haj Guide

Haj is the one of mandatory activity in Islam by the way of Prophet (peace be upon him). Haj can be performed in three types.

  • HAJJ TAMATHU = PERFORMING UMRAH THEN HAJJ
  • HAJJ IFRAD = PERFORMING ONLY HAJJ
  • HAJJ QIRAN = PERFORMING UMRAH HAJJ TOGETHER

THE RITUALS OF HAJJ

  • IHARAM
  • MAKING AN INTENSIO
  • TAKE A BATH
  • APPLY PERFUME
  • AND WEAR THE IHRAM CLOTH
  • AND OFFER TWO RAAKATH PRAYER
  • On the 10th day, Hajj the pilgrims slaughters the sacrificial animal And cuts or shave his hair and does the tawaaf and saai and back to mina and stay for two days and stone the zumaraath for two days.
